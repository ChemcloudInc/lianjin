Cambridge Chemicals is a second-to-none chemical supplier positioned to meet the needs of Chemical, agricultural and pharmaceutical industries with over 10,000 compounds in stock.

Our key business includes:

1. Intermediates for pharmaceutical and material research

2. Custom synthesis

3. Contract research

The intermediates for pharmaceutical and material research are our base. Custom synthesis is always another key component of our business. Our highly experienced scientists synthesize thousands of new compounds for hundreds of companies and applications from milligram to multi-kilo scale while meeting varies of specifications. We can make compounds over 30 complicated steps. We can also provide up to 100 kg of specific compounds with high purity requirements of > 99.5% and identify single impurity over 0.1%.

We build our reputation for high standard and on time delivery. The quality control is always an essential element to our customer as well as on-time delivery.

Our Contract Research Organization (CRO) agreements provide services for various companies. We have successfully helped a few of our customers to develop lead compounds into clinical or process studies. Our smart scientists continue providing unique designs and compounds which mostly are not available from any other suppliers in the world.

It's our assured ability and responsibility to stand with our customers and provide them with high quality services and compounds at competitive cost.

But, we are not only a chemical supplier. We provide comprehensive services and supports to the customers in drug design, MedChem and informatics, bioassay screening, material design, development of synthetic route, process modification, purification of natural products and chiral compounds, precise analysis, clinical CRO contact, shipping and handling, EHS and regulations, lab safety and experiment trainings, instrument sales and maintenance, procurement, international import and export support, oversea chemical stock, international business development etc.

Have a request? Call us or send email. Our specialist will always stand with you together.
